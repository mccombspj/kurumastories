Isuzu Bellett

Isuzu Bellett 1600GTR (1971)

Publication: Car Graphic
Format: Road Test
Date: June 1971
Author: “C/G Test Group” (uncredited)

Summary: The ultimate Bellett GT. In exchange for a stiff ride at low speeds and excessive noise at high speeds, the performance and maneuverability are the best in the class, the steering is quick and unmatched, the power-off oversteer caused by the rear swing axle is moderately suppressed, it is extremely fast and exciting on winding roads, and even with limited space in the back seat, it is a very good car. It is effectively a 2+2. The poor ventilation and heating system shows the age of the design.
